# Strategic Success Roadmap: 2026-2028

**Vision**: To be the default "Tourist Mover" in Southeast Asia's island destinations.

---

## Year 1: The Cebu Blueprint (2026)

* **Q1**: **Launch Pilot** (5 Units). Prove unit economics.
* **Q2**: **Operations Tuning**. Perfect the cleaning/charging cycle.
* **Q3**: **Fleet Expansion**. Reinvest profits + Debt to reach 20 units.
* **Q4**: **The "Holiday Test"**. Handle the Sinulog/Christmas peak demand perfectly.

## Year 2: Island Hopping (2027)

* **New Market 1**: **Bohol (Panglao)**.
  * *Why*: High tourist density, expensive tricycles, perfect range for EVs.
  * *Hub*: New Bohol International Airport <-> Alona Beach.
* **New Market 2**: **Siargao**.
  * *Why*: Surfers need clean, rack-equipped transport.
  * *Pivot*: Deploy "Rugged" Seagulls (Roof racks for boards).

## Year 3: The Capital & Beyond (2028)

* **The Manila Entry**: Enter BGC (Bonifacio Global City).
  * *Strategy*: Corporate shuttles for high-end offices.
* **International**: **Bali, Indonesia**.
  * *Why*: Similar topology to Cebu. High tourist volume. Chaos needs order.

## Critical Success Factors (KPIs)

1. **Utilization**: Must maintain >70% active rental time per car.
2. **Safety**: Zero major accidents (Smart Safety features of BYD).
3. **Brand Love**: Net Promoter Score (NPS) > 70.
