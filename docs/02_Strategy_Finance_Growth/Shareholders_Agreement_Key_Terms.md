# Shareholders' Agreement (SHA): Key Terms Sheet

**Company**: Cebu Loop Mobility Corp
**Parties**: Brian Shukri (Founder/Treasurer), Joy (Co-Founder/President)

---

## 1. Ownership Structure (Initial)

* **Authorized Capital Stock**: ₱10,000,000
* **Subscribed Capital**: ₱2,500,000
  * **Brian**: 60% (Controlling Interest)
  * **Joy**: 40% (Sweat Equity / Ops)
* **Paid-Up Capital**: ₱625,000 (25% of Subscribed)

## 2. Board Composition & Control

* **Board Seats**: 5 Directors
  * Brian's Nominees: 3
  * Joy's Nominees: 2
* **Quorum**: Requires presence of Brian (or his proxy).
* **Reserved Matters (Veto Rights)**: Brian has absolute veto power over:
  * Issuance of new shares (Dilution).
  * Incurring debt > ₱100,000.
  * Selling the company or assets.
  * Changing the primary business purpose.
  * Hiring/Firing C-Level Executives.

## 3. Anti-Dilution & Pre-Emptive Rights

* **Pre-Emptive Rights**: Existing shareholders have the first right of refusal to buy any new shares issued to maintain their percentage ownership.
* **Anti-Dilution**: If shares are issued at a lower valuation in the future (Down Round), conversion rates will adjust to protect early equity (Weighted Average method).

## 4. Transfer of Shares

* **Right of First Refusal (ROFR)**: If Joy wants to sell her shares, she must offer them to Brian first before any third party.
* **Lock-up Period**: Founders cannot sell shares for 3 years (Vesting Period) to ensure commitment.

## 5. Exit Mechanisms

* **Drag-Along Rights**: If Brian finds a buyer for >50% of the company, he can force other shareholders to sell their shares at the same price (prevents minority from blocking a buyout).
* **Tag-Along Rights**: If Brian sells his shares, Joy has the right to sell her shares to the same buyer at the same price (protects minority from being left behind).

## 6. Dispute Resolution

* **Venue**: Arbitration in Singapore (SIAC) or Courts of Cebu City (depending on preference).
* **Deadlock**: In case of a 50/50 deadlock (unlikely given board structure), the Chairman (Brian) casts the deciding vote.
