# Financial Controls & Banking Protocols

**Subject**: Protection of Capital & Expenditure Rules
**Authority**: Brian Shukri (Treasurer / Majority Owner)

---

## 1. Banking Architecture

### Primary Account (Operations)

* **Bank**: UnionBank of the Philippines
* **Type**: Corporate Checking Account
* **Signatories**:
  * **Sole Signatory**: Brian Shukri (Digital Approval via Token/App)
  * **View-Only Access**: Joy (For bookkeeping and preparation)

### Secondary Account (Petty Cash Cards)

* **Mechanism**: GCash / Maya Corporate Wallets linked to Fleet Runners.
* **Limit**: Max balance of ₱5,000 per runner for emergency charging/tolls.
* **Top-up Rule**: Only Brian can authorize top-ups from the Main Account.

## 2. The "Brian Veto" (Expenditure Limits)

To prevent runway burn, the following limits are hard-coded into company policy:

| Expense Amount | Required Approval |
| :--- | :--- |
| **< ₱1,000** | Joy (Auto-approved for Ops) |
| **₱1,000 - ₱10,000** | Joy (Requires Viber Notification) |
| **> ₱10,000** | **BRIAN MUST APPROVE** (Digital Sign-off) |
| **Any Capital Asset** | **BRIAN MUST APPROVE** |

## 3. Reimbursement Protocol

* **The "No Receipt, No Pay" Rule**: This is absolute.
* **Process**:
    1. Joy takes photo of receipt immediately.
    2. Uploads to Google Drive > `Finance/Receipts/YYYY-MM`.
    3. Logs into Excel Tracking Sheet.
    4. Reimbursement is processed every Friday.

## 4. Loan Repayment Mechanism

* **Priority**: The ₱1.75M Loan from Brian is Senior Debt.
* **Sweep**: At the end of every month, 100% of Net Income (Cash on Hand - Ops Reserve) is transferred to Brian's personal US account until the debt is cleared.
* **Ops Reserve**: The company shall maintain a minimum cash buffer of ₱50,000 at all times.
