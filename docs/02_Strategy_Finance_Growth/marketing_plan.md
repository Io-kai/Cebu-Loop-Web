# Pre-Launch Marketing Plan

**Objective**: Build an audience of 500+ qualified leads before Grand Launch
**Timeline**: Phase 0 through Phase 4 (Weeks 0-9)
**Budget**: ₱15,000 total

---

## 1. Email List Building (Week 0-8)

### Landing Page Capture

- **Action**: Add email signup form to cebuloop.com Coming Soon page
- **Tool**: Mailchimp Free Tier (up to 500 contacts)
- **Incentive**: "Be first to ride. Get 50% off your first Loop."
- **Goal**: 200 signups before launch

### QR Code Distribution

- **Action**: Print 100 QR code flyers linking to signup page
- **Placement**: IT Park cafes, Ayala food court, Marco Polo lobby
- **Cost**: ₱500 (printing)

---

## 2. Social Media Presence (Week 2+)

### Instagram Account: @cebuloop

- **Content Strategy**:
  - BYD Seagull beauty shots (free, use phone)
  - "Coming Soon" countdown stories
  - Behind-the-scenes: fleet arrival, branding, charging stations
  - Reels: "Why EVs are the future of Cebu"
- **Posting Cadence**: 3x/week minimum
- **Goal**: 500 followers by launch

### Facebook Page: Cebu Loop Mobility

- **Content**: Mirror Instagram + longer posts on pricing/benefits
- **Join Groups**: "Cebu Expats", "Cebu City Commuters", "IT Park Community"
- **Goal**: 300 likes by launch

---

## 3. Influencer Partnerships (Week 6-7)

### Micro-Influencer Campaign

- **Target**: Cebu lifestyle/travel bloggers with 5k-20k followers
- **Offer**: Free 1-day rental in exchange for Instagram post + story
- **Budget**: ₱0 (barter), or ₱2,000-3,000/post if paid
- **Goal**: 3 influencer posts during soft launch week

### Suggested Influencers to Contact

- Cebu Fashion Bloggers
- IT Park/Ayala-based content creators
- Hotel concierge partnerships (Marco Polo, Waterfront)

---

## 4. PR & Media (Week 7-8)

### Press Release

- **Story Angle**: "Cebu's First Premium Electric Car Rental Launches"
- **Outlets**:
  - Sun.Star Cebu
  - Cebu Daily News
  - Rappler (Tech section)
  - When in Cebu (online)
- **Distribution**: Email press release to editors + post on social
- **Cost**: ₱0 (organic)

### Media Day Event

- **Concept**: Invite 5 local journalists for free test drive Airport-to-IT Park
- **Date**: 2 days before Grand Launch
- **Budget**: ₱3,000 (snacks, parking, fuel)

---

## 5. Partnership Marketing (Week 4+)

### Hotel Concierge Program

- **Pitch**: Partner hotels offer "Cebu Loop Airport Transfer" to guests
- **Commission**: 10% referral fee to hotel
- **Targets**: Marco Polo, Waterfront, Radisson Blu, Seda

### Hospital/Corporate Program

- **Pitch**: Discounted rates for staff/patient families needing transport
- **Targets**: Chong Hua, Cebu Doctors, Ayala IT Park companies

---

## 6. Launch Events

### Soft Launch (Week 8)

- **Scope**: Invite-only (email list + influencers)
- **Discount**: 50% off first ride
- **Goal**: 20 bookings, gather feedback

### Grand Launch (Week 9)

- **Scope**: Public announcement
- **Promo**: "Launch Week Special" - 30% off all bookings
- **Social Push**: All channels announce simultaneously
- **Goal**: 50 bookings in first week
