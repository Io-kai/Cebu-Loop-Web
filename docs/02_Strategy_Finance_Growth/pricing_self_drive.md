# Self-Drive Rental Pricing Structure

**Effective**: Launch Date (Q1 2026)
**Vehicle**: BYD Seagull (2025 Model) - 5-Seater Electric Hatchback
**Range**: 305km (NEDC) | ~250km Real-World

---

## 1. Standard Rates

| Duration | Rate | Notes |
|----------|------|-------|
| **Hourly** | ₱200/hour | 3-hour minimum (₱600) |
| **Half-Day** | ₱800 | 6 hours max |
| **Full-Day** | ₱1,500 | 24-hour period |
| **Weekend** | ₱2,500 | Fri 6PM - Mon 9AM |
| **Weekly** | ₱7,000 | 7 consecutive days |

---

## 2. What's Included

- ✅ **Comprehensive Insurance** (Own Damage, Theft, Acts of Nature)
- ✅ **Roadside Assistance** (24/7 Hotline)
- ✅ **Full Battery Charge** at pickup
- ✅ **RFID for CCLEX/Expressway** (Tolls billed separately at cost)

---

## 3. Additional Fees

| Item | Cost | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| **Security Deposit** | ₱5,000 | Refunded within 3 days if no damage |
| **Late Return** | ₱300/hour | After 30-minute grace period |
| **Low Battery Penalty** | ₱500 | If returned below 20% charge |
| **Cleaning Fee** | ₱500-1,500 | Excessive dirt, pet hair, smoke odor |
| **Toll Reimbursement** | At Cost | RFID charges forwarded to customer |

---

## 4. Damage Excess (Deductible)

In case of damage, customer pays first:

| Damage Type | Customer Excess |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Minor (Scratches, Dents) | ₱3,000 |
| Major (Collision, Glass) | ₱10,000 |
| Total Loss / Theft | ₱25,000 |

> Insurance covers remainder. Excess waived if third-party is clearly at fault.

---

## 5. Eligibility Requirements

- ✅ Valid Driver's License (LTO Professional or Non-Pro)
- ✅ Minimum Age: 21 years
- ✅ Minimum License Holding: 1 year
- ✅ Valid Credit Card or GCash (for deposit hold)
- ✅ Philippine National or Valid Visa (for foreigners)
